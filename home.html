THE BOYS' BRIGADE NIGERIA

The World's First
Uniform Christain Voluntary Organization

The Boys Brigade was founded in glasgow on 4th october 1883 by sir Williams Alexander Smith. From this One Company formed in Scotland the BB Has Grown in to a Worldwide movement having worked with millions of children and young people for well over a century.
